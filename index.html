ABOUT FAFORLIFE

Faforlife is a multinational company founder by three doctors which are medical practitioners from Nigeria, India and China. The company liquid produces stemcell based therapies and other organic health products, the flagship product of the company is called FAFORON (AKA the miracle bottle).


WHAT IS FAFORON?

Our multi-cure stem cell dietary supplement (Faforlife) is made from 100% natural plant stemcells, the ingredients are locally sourced fro in Nigeria, India and China. Faforon has has been in use since 2002 with thousands of testimonials from health practitioners. We are the first producers of liquid based stemcell in the world, our products are highly potent and very affordable.
